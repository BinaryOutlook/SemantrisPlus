## Design Context

### Users
Browser word-game players dropping into short, replayable sessions. They want a quick semantic puzzle they can understand instantly, replay often, and enjoy in a single browser tab without setup friction.

### Brand Personality
Calm, sharp, playful.

The interface should feel composed and precise rather than loud or theatrical. It should create confidence through clarity, then add a light sense of play through motion, pacing, and tactile word interactions.

### Aesthetic Direction
Modern minimalist puzzle product.

Favor a light-first editorial shell with a darker playfield that draws attention to the stack. Use crisp typography, disciplined spacing, restrained accent color, and clean surfaces. Avoid retro HUD tropes, cyberpunk styling, dashboard chrome, and decorative glass effects.

### Design Principles
1. The playfield is the hero.
2. Clarity wins before decoration.
3. One accent, used with discipline.
4. Motion should explain game state.
5. Copy should stay short, human, and replay-friendly.
