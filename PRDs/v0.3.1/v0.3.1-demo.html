Cupertino Theme Direction

One clue. Three modes. One quieter, sharper shell.

This demo is a visual briefing for the planned Cupertino theme: black and light-gray environments, tight typography, blue-only interaction accents, restrained surfaces, and a playfield that stays dominant over the chrome.
