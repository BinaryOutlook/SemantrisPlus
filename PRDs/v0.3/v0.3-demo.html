What this demo is trying to lock in

v0.3 should standardize the product before it expands again.

The next implementation pass should use this design language to tighten product identity, clarify shared shell behavior, and improve replay and completion feedback without flattening the differences between modes.

Landing, mode selection, and in-game shells should feel like one coherent product family.
The board remains the hero. Supporting data should sit at the edges and stay legible without becoming chrome.
End-of-run states should feel earned and replay-friendly, not like a placeholder modal dropped on top of the game.
